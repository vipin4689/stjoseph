OUR AIM

St.Joseph's Matriculation Higher Secondary School aims at providing disciplined education according to Christian principles by bringing out the best and the noblest in the children to make citizens of good character. The management is the governing body of this esteemed institution. The Most Rev.Dr.L.Thomas Aquinas is the president of the school. He is also the highest authority who takes the final decision. His Excellency is also the Bishop of Coimbatore Diocese and his prayers, spiritual guidance, motivation and interest towards the school enriches the growth of the institution. St.Joseph's Matriculation Higher Secondary School was founded in the year 1986. R.C Mission of Coimbatore Diocese runs it. The Government since July 1986 recognized the school. It was upgraded as Higher Secondary School in October 1999. We have completed Silver Jubilee ERA.The Historic first step of the journey, towards excellence was taken up with 150 students and 3 teachers, was inaugurated by the Most Rev.Dr.M.Ambrose D.D.D.C.L the Bishop of Coimbatore. Today, the school has 2217 students and around 82 staff members.
